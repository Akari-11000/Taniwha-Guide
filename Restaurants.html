Restaurants

「 Highest Reviewed Restaurants in Christchurch, New Zealand 」

The Sign of the Takahe

The Sign of the Takahe is magnificent in all its grandeur and glamour, the location of this newly dedicated Functions & Wedding space with a welcoming casual and cozy “walk in for cabinet food” Cafe & Bar downstairs makes this unique venue one for everyone’s bucket list!

Ilex Botanic Gardens & Café

Ilex is an architectural delight that pays homage to its natural surroundings. Walking past the nursery and into the cafe, you're greeted with a flood of natural light, minimalist decor, and lots of green. The floor to ceiling windows provides an unobstructed view of monumental trees, lush lawn and tropical glasshouse beyond. Centered on local produce through simple, sophisticated dishes, Ilex’s menu is brimming with memorable breakfast, brunch and lunch dishes. A must-do spot for the warmer months.

Evil Genius Ferrymead

One of Ferrymead’s best-kept secrets, Evil Genius Bar & Eatery is a destination you need to get to know. Open Wednesday to Sunday from noon ’til way past dusk, the intimate space offers relaxed dining, choice beverages, and live entertainment along the Heathcote Estuary. Grab a seat outside on the warmer nights and watch the nightlights reflect off the water. Pop in for a leisurely lunch, end the day with casual dining, or meet your best wine-drinking buddy here for a glass or two. Word has it they have some of the best fried chicken in the city.

Curators House Restaurant

Right at the edge of Botanic Gardens is the historic Curator's House. Framed by elegant landscaping with a view over the Ōtākaro Avon River, you can't beat a lazy Summer's lunch here. Once housing the Botanic Garden's Curator's and their families until 1983, the building now serves traditional Spanish cuisine after the owner's homeland. Visitors are encouraged to wander through the building and its garden, and enjoy summer dining al fresco in the garden or fireside in winter. Be treated to fresh local produce including goods from their very own garden and savour their beautifully crafted wine list.

Mr.Brightside Rooftop Bar

The penthouse-style rooftop bar on The Terrace is the perfect spot to get above it all. Resting atop the iconic Public Trust Office, the views take in the Ōtākaro Avon River and Cathedral square below, with views of the Port Hills and Southern Alps beyond. Relax with a drink in hand and enjoy the bright side of life. The retractable roof lets you bask in the summer sun till twilight creeps over the city. Savour a cocktail after work alongside their gourmet platters. Be sure to arrive early or reserve ahead of time. Mr Brightside is a local favourite.
