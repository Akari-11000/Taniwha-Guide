Top 5 Hotels in Christchurch

The George

9.4 rating for a two-person stay.

Centrally located in pretty Christchurch, overlooking Hagley Park and the River Avon, the George is a luxurious 5-star boutique hotel. Guests enjoy free Wi-Fi. The George, a member of Small Luxury Hotels of the World, has 53 stylish and comfortable guest rooms. All of the accommodations are spacious, feature contemporary furnishings and have a modern bathroom. Guests can enjoy a great range of modern in-room amenities including a 42” LCD TV, digital movie system and complimentary bathroom toiletries. 50 Bistro is an award-winning restaurant at the property, which serves contemporary cuisine and also offers al fresco dining on the terrace. The George is within easy reach of the city centre and its arts centre, restaurants and theatres. Christchurch Botanic Gardens is also on your doorstep. Christchurch Casino is just a 3-minute walk away. The George has repeatedly been recognised as 'New Zealand's Leading Boutique Hotel' from 2006-2010 and 2012-2020.

The Observatory Hotel Christchurch

9.8 rating for a two-person stay.

The Observatory Hotel Christchurch is ideally located in Christchurch. It features a buffet breakfast and free WiFi throughout the property. This 5-star hotel offers a 24-hour front desk and a concierge service. Private parking is available on site. The Observatory Hotel Christchurch offers air-conditioned rooms with a wardrobe, a coffee machine, a minibar, a safety deposit box and a flat-screen TV. The private bathroom has a shower. Each room includes bed linen and towels. The Observatory Hotel Christchurch also offers a fitness centre. The area is popular for cycling, and The Observatory Hotel Christchurch also offers bike hire and car hire. Popular points of interest near the accommodation include Canterbury Museum, Christchurch Art Gallery and Hagley Park. The nearest airport is Christchurch International Airport, 10 km from The Observatory Hotel Christchurch.

The Mayfair

9.1 rating for a two-person stay.

Located in Christchurch, 1 km from Canterbury Museum, The Mayfair provides accommodation with a fitness centre, private parking, a restaurant and a bar. This 5-star hotel offers room service, a 24-hour front desk and free WiFi. The property is 1.2 km from the city centre and 900 metres from Christchurch Art Gallery. At The Mayfair, every room comes with air conditioning, a seating area, a flat-screen satellite TV, a safety deposit box and a private bathroom with a shower, free toiletries and a hairdryer. You will find a kettle in the room, while some rooms have a balcony. Other options offer mountain views. At the property, rooms come with bed linen and towels. A à la carte, continental or Full English/Irish breakfast is served each morning. Popular points of interest near The Mayfair include Hagley Park, Victoria Square and Bridge of Remembrance. The nearest airport is Christchurch International Airport, 9 km from The Mayfair.

Wyndham Garden Christchurch Kilmore Street

9.1 rating for a two-person stay.

Wyndham Garden Christchurch Kilmore Street is conveniently located in Christchurch and offers air-conditioned rooms, a fitness center, free WiFi and a restaurant. This 4-star hotel offers room service and a 24-hour front desk. Private parking is available on site. All rooms include a private bathroom with free toiletries, while some rooms also have a kitchen with a dishwasher. At Wyndham Garden Christchurch Kilmore Street, rooms include a TV and a hairdryer. Breakfast options include à la carte, continental and Full English/Irish each morning. Guests at the accommodation will be able to enjoy activities in and around Christchurch, including hiking and skiing. Popular points of interest near Wyndham Garden Christchurch Kilmore Street include Christchurch Art Gallery, Canterbury Museum and Hagley Park. The nearest airport is Christchurch International Airport, 10 km from Wyndham Garden Christchurch Kilmore Street.

Commodore Airport Hotel Christchurch

9.5 rating for a two-person stay.

Just 5 minutes from Christchurch International Airport, this hotel offers a free 24-hour airport shuttle, an indoor pool, hot tub, sauna and fitness centre. Commodore Airport Hotel Christchurch features spacious rooms, meeting and conference facilities and free parking. The award-winning native gardens provide a tranquil setting. Each room has natural light and a balcony or garden access. Workspaces include a large desk, telephones with voicemail and modem points with high-speed internet access. Patterson's Restaurant serves seasonal cuisine with European, Asian and Pacific influences. Guests at The Bar can enjoy a drink on the terrace or watch a game on the 70-inch plasma TV. Commodore Airport Hotel Christchurch is just a 15-minute drive from Christchurch City Centre.
