「 Think To Do in Christchurch, New Zealand 」

Gondola ride

An unmissable Christchurch experience, the Christchurch Gondola offers stunning 360-degree views of the city, Lyttelton Harbour and beyond from the summit station situated atop the Port Hills. See the snow-capped Southern Alps, sprawling Canterbury Plains and the sparkling waters of Pegasus Bay and the Pacific Ocean.

Visit Orana Wildlife Park.

Orana Wildlife Park specialises in getting visitors up close and personal with the animals. Visitors can hand feed giraffe, meet rhino up close, see a tiger search for food and much more!
An extensive collection of New Zealand native species is of particular appeal for visitors. Observe New Zealand kiwi, tuatara and a range of endangered birds. Orana Wildlife Park has one of the most comprehensive public displays of native geckos. A farmyard area enables visitors to pet a range of domestic animals.

Rafting at Southern White Water

White water rafting is a thrilling and adventurous activity that appeals to both adrenaline junkies and nature enthusiasts. At Southern White Water, we offer an array of rafting experiences that cater to all levels, from beginners to seasoned rafters, and offer packages for families, friends, and even corporate groups looking for team-building activities. Our expert guides ensure that every trip is memorable, educational, and, most importantly, fun.

Hammer Springs Bungy

Take the plunge with an adrenaline-inducing Hanmer Springs Bungy Jump! Experience the elation from a 35-metre freefall to the surging river below amidst stunning scenery at Canterbury's only bungy site.

Skiing in Mount Hutt

Mt Hutt boasts the longest ski season in New Zealand. Nearby Porters is home to Castle Hill, one of the top bouldering areas in the world.
